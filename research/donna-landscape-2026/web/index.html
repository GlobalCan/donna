Memory persistence shape

The memory axis is where Donna's tenets diverge most sharply from the field. Bitemporal stores (Zep / Graphiti / mnemostack) attach event-time and ingestion-time to every fact and invalidate rather than delete; agent-rewrite stores (Letta / Mem0) overwrite. Most products either default to per-conversation history or punt to RAG. Only three products in the entire scan implement bitemporal facts.
